Our Programs

Our programs center on community, bringing together participants from the U.S. and abroad through shared activities, learning, and play.

Pickleball for All Ages

Inclusive pickleball activities designed for players of all ages and skill levels. Whether you're picking up a paddle for the first time or looking for competitive play, our programs meet you where you are.

We offer beginner clinics, intermediate drills, advanced round-robins, and social play sessions throughout the week at multiple locations.

Get Started

Community Wellness

Programs aimed at promoting physical and mental health within the community. Pickleball is more than a sport — it's a path to better health, stronger social bonds, and an active lifestyle.

We partner with local organizations to offer wellness workshops, fitness assessments, and health-focused community events.

Learn More

International Exchanges

Experience different cultures and create lasting friendships through international exchange programs. We connect players across borders to share the joy of pickleball and build cross-cultural understanding.

Our exchange programs span the U.S., Taiwan, and China — bringing together communities through sport and shared experiences.

Learn More

Youth Camps

Enroll children in fun and educational youth camps focused on sports and character building. Our camps combine pickleball instruction with teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership development.

Camps run during summer and school breaks, with age-appropriate sessions for kids and teens.
