Our Programs

Each program is rooted in community — connecting people across the U.S. and internationally through sport, education, and cultural exchange.

Pickleball for All Ages

From first-time players to competitive athletes, our inclusive sessions welcome every age and ability level. No experience necessary — just bring your enthusiasm.

Weekly offerings include beginner clinics, intermediate drills, advanced round-robins, and social open-play sessions across multiple locations.

Get Started

Community Wellness

Pickleball is more than a game — it's a proven pathway to better physical health, stronger social connections, and a more active daily routine.

In partnership with local organizations, we host wellness workshops, fitness assessments, and health-focused community events throughout the year.

Learn More

International Exchanges

Our international exchange initiatives connect players across borders, fostering mutual respect and cross-cultural dialogue through the shared language of sport.

Active partnerships span the U.S., Taiwan, and China — enabling player visits, joint training sessions, and collaborative community events.

Learn More

Youth Camps

Our youth camps blend athletic instruction with character development — teaching teamwork, sportsmanship, and leadership through the lens of pickleball.

Sessions are offered during summer and school breaks, with age-appropriate programming for children and teens.
