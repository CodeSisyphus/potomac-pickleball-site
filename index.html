About Our Club

The Potomac Pickleball Club is a community-based organization dedicated to promoting active living, wellness, and social connection for people of all ages and skill levels in the Potomac area.

Founded by a USA Pickleball Association Ambassador, our club reflects a strong commitment to advancing the sport with national standards, community outreach, and player development.

We operate as a program of ActiveLife Foundation, Inc., a Maryland-based nonprofit organization.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible sports and wellness programs for youth, seniors, and families, using pickleball and related activities to strengthen community well-being and foster cross-cultural connection.

👁

Our Vision

A world where sports transcend boundaries — connecting people across age, culture, and nationality — and where playing together builds lasting friendships and meaningful human connections.
