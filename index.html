About Our Club

Potomac Pickleball Club is a nonprofit organization committed to promoting active living, holistic wellness, and meaningful social connection for individuals of all ages and abilities throughout the greater Potomac region.

Founded by a USA Pickleball Association Ambassador, we uphold national standards in player development, community outreach, and competitive play.

We operate as an official program of ActiveLife Foundation, Inc., a registered Maryland nonprofit.

Our Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To deliver accessible sports and wellness programming for youth, seniors, and families — leveraging pickleball as a platform to strengthen community health and cultivate cross-cultural understanding.

👁

Our Vision

A world where sport bridges every boundary — age, culture, and nationality — and where playing together forges lasting friendships and genuine human connection.
